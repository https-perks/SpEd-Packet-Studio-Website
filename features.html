A closer look

From the IEP to a packet
staff can actually use.

Copy over the information you already have, organize it once, and let each step build on the last.

01
Student setup

Keep the foundation together.

Enter student details, case manager information, related-service contacts, accommodations, service areas, minutes, settings, and packet audiences in one connected profile.

  • Structured student and service information
  • Accommodations and behavior support pages
  • Audience selection for each project
02
At-a-glance and goals

Turn IEP language into clear staff guidance.

Copy and paste existing IEP information, then organize it into an easy-to-scan instructional summary. Keep complete goal statements alongside concise summaries that flow into data sheets.

  • Reorderable at-a-glance sections
  • Full IEP goals plus staff-friendly summaries
  • Goals connected to service areas
03
Data and observations

Build practical tools around the goals.

Create progress-monitoring sheets without copying goal language into another file. Choose templates, attach goals, set schedules, customize columns, and build separate observation pages for classroom notes.

  • Reusable data-sheet templates
  • Goal-linked summaries and custom tables
  • Observation logs and staff checklists
04
Packet versions

Decide what each audience receives.

Select a version, show or hide its pages, and arrange them in the order that makes sense for that reader. A general education teacher, case manager, and coach can each receive a focused packet from the same source information.

  • Audience-specific page visibility
  • Independent page order for every version
  • One source of truth across all packets
